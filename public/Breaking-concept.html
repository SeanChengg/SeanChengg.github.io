Breaking Bad

01
Concept

Breaking Bad-themed Halloween project inspired by Walter White's trunk-mounted M60.

Design Features
  • Modified Gatling gun model equipped with a DC motor to spin gun barrels.
  • RC servo-driven 5-bar linkage mimics machine gun pivoting by a repurposed garage door opener.
  • IR remote (car key trigger), speed-adjusting potentiometer, and gunfire sound effects from a speaker.
