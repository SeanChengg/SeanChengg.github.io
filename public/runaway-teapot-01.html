The Runaway Teapot

Project Overview: The Runaway Teapot is a mechatronic project featuring a mobile teapot platform with avoidance and notification features. It uses mecanum wheels, DC motors, a Raspberry Pi Pico, and more.

  • DC motors, Mecanum Wheels, IR Remote Controller
  • Raspberry Pi Pico, VL53L0X Laser sensors
  • Integrated timer for optimal tea steeping
  • Auditory alert when tea is ready
  • Runs away if picked up too early
  • Remote repositioning via IR
